# OneTracker Platform Features

OneTracker Technologies offers a powerful suite of logistics intelligence tools.

## 1. Unified Shipment Dashboard

Monitor all shipments across multiple carriers in a single centralized dashboard.

- Real-time status updates
- Smart filtering & search
- Bulk shipment management

## 2. AI Delay Prediction

Our AI engine analyzes historical route data, weather conditions, and carrier performance to predict potential delivery delays.

## 3. Smart Route Analytics

Gain insights into:

- Average transit times
- Carrier performance benchmarks
- Regional delivery efficiency
- Bottleneck detection

## 4. Automated Notifications

Notify customers automatically via:

- Email
- SMS
- WhatsApp

Notifications can be triggered based on:
- Shipment dispatched
- Out for delivery
- Delay detected
- Delivery completed

## 5. Booking & Demo Scheduling System

OneTracker includes a timezone-aware demo booking system where users can:

- Select timezone
- Choose available slots
- Receive automated confirmation emails

All booking times are stored securely and validated against availability.
